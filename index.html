Selected Work

★ Featured · Machine Learning · Healthcare

Early Detection of Sepsis in ICU Patients

Built a full ML pipeline to detect sepsis early in ICU patients — a condition responsible for millions of deaths annually. The core challenge: only 7.27% of patients develop sepsis, so naive models simply predict "no sepsis" for everyone and call it 92% accuracy. We tackled this with SMOTE oversampling + class-weighted classifiers across seven algorithm families. Random Forest came out on top.
